Background

In my Interaction Design 2 – Mobile course, our second project was to create a mobile app for an elderly audience. I decided to design a travel app, keeping in mind that older travelers may have additional needs that younger travelers do not have to consider. Tourzy is a “virtual tour guide” who helps you plan activities for your travels based on personal preferences and needs. The app responds to in-the-moment needs and makes suggestions during your travels.
