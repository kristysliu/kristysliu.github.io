A.I. Chatbot for Mall

Task

Create a pitch deck for AMK Hub on how implementing chatbot technology on their mall's website and Facebook Page can improve customer service.

User Research

Our team spoke with 3 Singaporean locals in order to better understand shoppers' needs and their mall experiences. Using the interviews, 2 user personas were created to guide the chatbot's features.

Pain points:
  1. Mall parking tickets are tedious to obtain (visitors have to wait in a queue with their purchase receipt to get free parking).
  2. The store directory is confusing.
  3. Searching for specific stores and products is inefficient.
Process & Iterations

1: Brainstormed 10 possible functions, and outlined the 5 main categories that will appear when opening the chatbot: directions, directory, scanning store QR code, WiFi access, and further help.

2: Modified the categories to 5 selections that will appear on the slider of buttons and drafted functions, initial dialogue flow, and potential questions the user could ask.

3: Modified the categories to 5 selections that will appear on the slider of buttons and drafted functions, initial dialogue flow, and potential questions the user could ask.

Solution

Constructed a pitch deck and dialogue flow for a chatbot with 8 key features: directions, item and store search, ordering food and beverage, favorites, entertainment and cinema, promotions, parking, and WiFi. Finalized a demo for AMK Hub and videos showing 2 user interactions with one user, who prefers being guided by buttons and another user who prefers a conversational approach.

As part of our solution, we also highlighted the benefits of implementing a chatbot versus a mobile application.
